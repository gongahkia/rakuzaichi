Rakuzaichi for Safari

Export conversations from supported AI chat sites into local files on this Mac.

Extension status

You can turn on Rakuzaichi in Safari Extensions settings.

Rakuzaichi is enabled. You can turn it off in Safari Extensions settings.

Rakuzaichi is disabled. You can turn it on in Safari Extensions settings.

What it does

Rakuzaichi detects supported chat pages, reads the visible conversation, and lets you export CSV, TSV, JSON, NDJSON, XML, or YAML files.

Permissions and website access

The extension requests access only to the supported chat domains plus local storage and alarms for extension settings and optional auto-export scheduling.

Privacy

Rakuzaichi does not send telemetry or remote analytics. Settings, export history, and auto-export status stay on-device. Exported files are saved only when you trigger the browser save flow.

Support scope

Supported sites in this build: ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, DeepSeek, Grok, Copilot, Mistral Le Chat, and HuggingChat.
